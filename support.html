2. Common Questions

How do I log a race?
Tap the "+" button on the home screen and select "Log Race." Fill in the details for your session and tap Save.

How do I log a part change?
Tap the "+" button on the home screen and select "Log Part Change." Add a description and optionally attach a photo.

Can I edit a race after saving?
Editing is not available in the current version. Entries are read-only once saved.

How do I delete my account?
Go to your Profile screen and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your account and all associated data, including race logs and photos.

Can I use RACELOGR for multiple karts or drivers?
Yes. You can add multiple karts and drivers from within the app and associate them with each race log.

My photos aren't uploading — what should I do?
Check your internet connection and ensure you've granted the app camera or photo library permission in your device Settings. If the issue persists, email us.
