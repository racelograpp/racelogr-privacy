Overview This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the RACE.LOGR mobile application. RACE.LOGR allows users to log and store data related to dirt kart racing events for personal reference. All race data entered into the App is private to the user and is not publicly visible. 2. Information We Collect We collect the following categories of information: A. Account Information Email address Encrypted password (via authentication provider) Email addresses are used as the account identifier (no separate username is collected). We do not collect: Full legal names Usernames separate from email Profile photos Mailing addresses Phone numbers B. User-Generated Race Log Data Users may voluntarily enter the following information: Track name Driver name (free-text entry) Lap times Kart setup specifications Weather conditions Notes Photos related to race events This information is private and accessible only to the authenticated user. Users may enter any name or free-text information at their discretion. C. Photos If users upload photos, they are stored securely in cloud storage and associated only with the user's account. D. Crash Logs We may collect crash log data if the App experiences an error. Crash logs may include: Device type Operating system version Technical diagnostic information Error reports Crash logs are used solely to improve app stability and performance. We do not use crash logs for advertising or tracking. 3. Information We Do Not Collect We do not collect: Precise location data IP addresses for tracking purposes Advertising identifiers Payment information Health data Biometric data Contacts Browsing history We do not use analytics tools at this time. 4. How We Use Your Information We use collected information only to: Create and manage user accounts Authenticate users Store and retrieve race log data Store user-uploaded photos Provide customer support Improve app reliability through crash diagnostics We do not sell user data. We do not use data for advertising. We do not send promotional or marketing emails. 5. Third-Party Service Providers We use the following service providers to operate the App: Supabase Supabase provides backend infrastructure including: Authentication (email and password login) Database storage Secure photo storage Supabase servers are located in the United States. Supabase processes data on our behalf in accordance with their privacy and security practices. GitHub GitHub is used solely for source code management and does not process user data. We do not use third-party advertising networks. 6. Data Storage and Security We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect user data. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Authentication credentials are securely managed. Access to backend systems is restricted. Photos and race data are stored in secure cloud infrastructure. While we take reasonable steps to protect data, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure. 7. Data Retention We retain user data only for as long as the account remains active. If a user deletes their account: All associated account data Race logs Uploaded photos will be permanently deleted from active systems without undue delay. We do not retain user data for marketing purposes. 8. Account Deletion Users may delete their account directly within the App. Upon deletion: Access is revoked immediately. Associated race logs and photos are permanently removed. If assistance is needed, users may contact racelogr.app@gmail.com . 9. Children's Privacy RACE.LOGR is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us. 10. U.S. Users RACE.LOGR is intended for users located in the United States. All data is processed and stored within the United States. 11. Your Rights You may: Access your stored race log data within the App Correct or modify entries at any time Delete your account and associated data Contact us with privacy-related questions To exercise any rights, contact racelogr.app@gmail.com . 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this document.
