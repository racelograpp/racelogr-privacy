1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the RACE.LOGR mobile application.

RACE.LOGR allows users to log and store data related to dirt kart racing events for personal reference. All race data entered into the App is private to the user and is not publicly visible.

2. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

We collect the following:

  • Email address
  • Encrypted password (via authentication provider)

Email addresses are used as the account identifier. No separate username is collected.

We do not collect:

  • Full legal names
  • Profile photos
  • Mailing addresses
  • Phone numbers

B. User-Generated Race Log Data

Users may voluntarily enter the following:

  • Track name
  • Driver name (free-text entry)
  • Lap times
  • Kart setup specifications
  • Weather conditions
  • Notes
  • Photos related to race events

This information is private and accessible only to the authenticated user.

C. Photos

If users upload photos, they are stored securely in cloud storage and associated only with the user's account.

D. Crash Logs

We may collect crash log data if the App experiences an error. Crash logs may include device type, operating system version, and technical diagnostic information. Crash logs are used solely to improve app stability and are never used for advertising or tracking.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Precise location data
  • IP addresses for tracking purposes
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Payment information
  • Health or biometric data
  • Contacts or browsing history

We do not use analytics tools at this time.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Authenticate users
  • Store and retrieve race log data
  • Store user-uploaded photos
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app reliability through crash diagnostics

We do not sell user data, use data for advertising, or send promotional emails.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

Supabase

Supabase provides our backend infrastructure, including authentication, database storage, and secure photo storage. Supabase servers are located in the United States. Supabase processes data on our behalf in accordance with their privacy and security practices.

GitHub

GitHub is used solely for source code management and does not process user data.

We do not use third-party advertising networks.

6. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect user data. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Photos and race data are stored in secure cloud infrastructure.

While we take reasonable steps to protect data, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as the account remains active. If a user deletes their account, all associated account data, race logs, and uploaded photos will be permanently deleted without undue delay. We do not retain user data for marketing purposes.

8. Account Deletion

Users may delete their account directly within the App. Upon deletion, access is revoked immediately and all associated race logs and photos are permanently removed.

If assistance is needed, contact racelogr.app@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

RACE.LOGR is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

10. U.S. Users

RACE.LOGR is intended for users located in the United States. All data is processed and stored within the United States.

11. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your stored race log data within the App
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

To exercise any rights, contact racelogr.app@gmail.com.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
